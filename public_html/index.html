Backpacking

To determine what you need to bring on a backpacking trip, think about how far you plan to hike, how remote the location is and what the weather forecast has in store. Consider these categories when getting ready for a trip backpacking gear ,clothing/footwear, camp kitchen , navigation ,food and water health hygiene, emergency items , and lastly tools for repairs to gear. This list include a couple of the bare minimums that are absolutely essential for a backing trip.

Essentials

  • Backpack
  • Backpacking tent
  • Sleeping bag
  • Headlamp/ flash light
  • Backpacking stove/kit and fuel
  • Water filtration kit :gravity filter or purifying tablets
  • Bear canister/food sack or hang bag
  • first aid kit
  • Emergency whistle
