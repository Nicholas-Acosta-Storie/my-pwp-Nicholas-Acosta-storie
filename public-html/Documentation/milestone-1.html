MilesStone 1

Purpose:

This project will be a website focused towards beginner or intermediate people that are interested in camping and hiking in new mexico and the information and essentials needed for different types of camping and hiking.

Audience:

This website is designed with a camping and hiking audience in mind, and is targeted to individuals that are seeking information about camping and hiking in new mexico. Target audience to include: Beginner and intermediate campers and hikers.

Goal:

The primary goal of this site is for users interested in camping and hiking in new mexico to contact me through the contact form, and/or the connected social media profiles For information about camping or hiking in new mexico or the essentails needed for camping and hiking. The secondary goal of this site is to showcase my work to potential employers, and to establish a professional online presence in fullstack Development.
